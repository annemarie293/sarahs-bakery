The sweetest place in town...

Hi, I'm Sarah!

Baking has always been a strong passion of mine and there's nothing more I love to do than whip up some beautiful fresh cakes to enjoy with my friends and family. I decided to take the plunge and turn my passion into my career this year, so if you're living in the greater Dublin area, now I can share my favourite treats with you too. All my cakes are made with local and organic ingredients, and I'm testing some new vegan and gluten free recipes, so watch this space for news! Check out my current selection below, or complete the order form if you'd like a custom celebration cake.
